Who we are.

Keiran Hoare

CEO & Founder of BIYONDATA​

Keiran founded BIYONDATA in 2021, after identifying a gap in market for small and mid-sized companies and their lack of experience in preparing to raise capital from institutional investors.​

Keiran has extensive financial markets experience, having worked in the industry for over 12 years.

Key skill set includes financial model, financial statement analysis, company valuation, investor pitch decks and strategy.​

Keiran has worked across both public and private equity markets. An equity research analyst at Moelis Australia (now MA Financial), covering small- cap ASX listed stocks, predominately in the tech space. Following on from this Keiran moved to the buy-side where he worked for Anacacia capital, a boutique fund manager, and Argyle Capital, a real-asset investment manager as an investment analyst.​

Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Business (Finance Major, IT Sub-Major)
  • UTS Sydney Graduate Certificate of Applied Finance​
